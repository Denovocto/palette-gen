How to use palette-gen

Usage:

  • Click on a color's HEX button to copy it to your clipboard in HEX format
  • Hover your mouse on a color to see the like and delete action buttons
  • Click on the heart to like a color make it persist when generating new colors
  • Click on the trashcan to delete the selected color from the palette

Keybindings:

  • g - Generate a new colors for the colors that aren't liked
  • e - Export the palette to clipboard
  • n - Add a new random color to the current palette up to a maximum of 10
